About this recipe

I chose this recipe because this is the first cookie learned how to make. Over the years I have tried to add my own spin to the cookies. But each year I get disaponted and go back to the classic cookie recpie. I first found this recipe in a old (from the 1970's) cook book. My mom refuses to give up the cook book until she dies. So i went to the intrent and found my go to sugar cookie recipe.

Every year, for every holiday I make this recipe even though i know I can go to the store and by the mix I still go through the the whole process of makeing them from scratch. Now that I have my own daughter her and I will make them toghether. We both think they taste better than the betty Crocker mix cookies.
