About this recipe

I chose this recipe because this is the first cookie learned how to make. Over the years I have tried to add my own spin to the cookies. But each year I get disaponted and go back to the classic cookie recpie. I first found this recipe in a old (from the 1970's) cook book when I was in Middle School. My mom refuses to give up the cookbook until she dies. So i went desided to go to the intrent and found my go to sugar cookie recipe.

Every year, for every holiday I make this recipe. It brings back lots of memories that i tend to forget about through out the year. Paired with a big glass of milk or during the christmas season some spiked egg nog makes me a very happy lady. I know that I can just go to the store and buy the mix I still go through the whole process of makeing them from scratch. Now that I have my own daughter her and I make them toghether. I feel that they taste better because of the memories we make together while we are baking.
